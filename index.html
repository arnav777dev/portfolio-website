01

Kisan Mitr

Kisan Mitr is an Android application designed to assist farmers in assessing nutrient levels in their soil for better crop management. Developed an interface to authenticate users and manage farmer data, storing and retrieving NPK values of soil from their fields. Enabled nutrient data collection by connecting soil sensors via USB, facilitating easy, cost-effective testing without an Arduino.

02

CanSat India 23

CanSat Development and Launch: An ISRO-Affiliated Success.
Securing 6th position in the prestigious ISRO CanSat India competition, we designed and launched a CanSat in Ahmedabad under the guidance of ISRO Chief S. Somanathan. The CanSat was engineered to transmit critical sensor data, including velocity and pressure, which was displayed in real time on the Ground Control Station (GCS). Leveraging technologies such as Python, PyQt5, Arduino, and C++, we ensured efficient data communication and monitoring, contributing to the success of the mission.

Home Automation Using Gestures

This project reimagines the interaction between individuals and their home environments by introducing a gesture recognition-based Home Automation system. With simple hand movements, users can effortlessly control various appliances, including chimneys, geysers, and TVs.
